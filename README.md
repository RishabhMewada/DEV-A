## What is DEV-A?
Dev-A is an devboard based on the RP2040 microcontroller , made for projects integration , while it is based on the rassberry pi pico

<img width="427" height="849" alt="KICAD" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c2971e7b-519d-4feb-93b0-5d8316301c43" />

## Features
- The RP2040 microcontrol capable of integrations into any mini hobby projects
- Type-c port capable of flashing into the emmmc fast and quick and efficent power supply
- Pin headers , can connect into any breadboard and jupper pins , making it easy for integration

## Parts

- RP2040 (Microcontroller)
- eMMC (Flash memory)
- charging ic ( voltage controller)
- Type-c female connector
- pin headers (2x20 pin , 1x3 pin)
- crystal 12Mhz 
- capacitors , resistors 
- push button
